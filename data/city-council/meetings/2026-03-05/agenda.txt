G. WORKSHOP DISCUSSION

1. Historic Inventory Nomination for 684 Broadway, Central Fire Station Workshop
   POSITION PAPER OF THE CITY MANAGER
   The City of South Portland created the South Portland Inventory of Archaeological and Historic Resources on July 16, 2019. The Historic Preservation Committee voted unanimously on August 19, 2025 to recommend adding the Central Fire Station at 684 Broadway to the Inventory. Note that inclusion would not prevent demolition of the existing station. Council is being asked whether or not it wishes to add Central Fire Station to the Inventory.

2. Mahoney/City Facilities/Public Safety Facilities
   POSITION PAPER OF THE CITY MANAGER
   Key questions for Council guidance:
   1) Does Council wish to continue with planned construction of a new Central Fire Station on site?
   2) Is Council ok with staff proceeding with a bond referendum for public safety facilities for November 2026?
   3) Can City staff begin looking at other locations for a combined City Hall/Hamlin, in addition to the A1++ option?
   4) Is the Council open to a split referendum?
