What is a Community School?
A Community School is a school that:
● Serves as a hub to respond to the unique needs of its
community
● Reduces barriers to school and achievement
● Integrates academics with health, social, and community
services
● Organizes the resources of the school, families, and
community around student success
Community Schools are a STRATEGY, not a PROGRAM.
Every Community School is different, serving the needs of
their students, families, staff, and community.

sessions (BBAB-R) in order to enable our student representatives to personally meet with the semi-finalists
